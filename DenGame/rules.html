Game Rules

Each team is allowed to book one task at a time, upon the completion of the booked task,
a team can book a new task for a maximum of two tasks a day.

In order to book a task, please send an email to br1group.operations@gmail.com, with the Subject as "Den Game".
In the email body please include:
1. ID of the task(s) that you wish to book.
2. Phone number of team leader.
3. Team Name.
4. Preferred date and time when the team can complete each task.
(Take into consideration the estimated time needed to complete the task.)

Bookings are done on a first come first served basis.

Booking Status Codes:
Available: Can be booked by anyone.
Unavailable: Cannot be booked, will be come Available in the future.
Pending: A team has requested to book the task.
Booked: A team's request to book a task has been accepted.
Completed: A team has completed a task.

Once a team has booked a task they are required to complete all the subtasks, on the specified date,
of the task to gain the total number of points.
Failing to complete a task to specification, the team will be granted points based on the number of subtasks completed to specification.

Booking Date is the date on which a team has agreed to complete a task.

Additional rules will be posted soon.
